There're two addresses for IPv6 loopback addresses.

- `::1`
- `::`

If you want to access the addresses from your browser, the URL is:

- `http://[::1]/`
- `http://[::]/`


## Reference

- [RFC 2732 - Format for Literal IPv6 Addresses in URL's](https://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc2732)
